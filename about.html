ABOUT

In spring 2024 I left a long career to do what I'd never had enough time for: to write, paint, make comics, make music, and read.

My reward system is oriented around the moment something clicks — developing ability rather than tangible results. I put in hard daily work and can observe a slow but steady increase in ability.

Tangible deliverables appear along the way — but as byproducts, not as goals. My actual goal is internal — the satisfaction of being able to do something that seemed impossible a month before.

I'm Swedish. I write fiction and poetry in Swedish, comics and correspondence in English.

This site is a record of the practice. The newsletter goes out a few times a year when there's something worth saying.

If you're making work for its own sake, and want to discuss ideas or offer honest critique in return for receiving it - write to me at jan@jansandstorm.com.
