Little Eagle Down - draft comic in print

The first four pages of the Little Eagle Down comic draft were printed in a collection together with the other students' drafts.

The biggest lesson I learned was the importance of separating what is important in each panel from the background and less important details when drawing.

I'm considering adding color to each panel to make it easier to see what is drawn.
