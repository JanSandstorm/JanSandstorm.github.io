COMICS ASSIGNMENT

In the comics creation course we are creating a 12-16 page comic from idea to print.

My story will be about a wild-life photography couple who find themselves trapped in the wilderness when their mobile home breaks down. Gradually it becomes clear that their engine problem was no accident and that the beautiful surroundings are hiding a deadly threat.
