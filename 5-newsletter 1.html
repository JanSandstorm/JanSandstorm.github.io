Newsletter #1, January 24, 2024


Time for the first newsletter as promised!

The blog is now up and running but I have not added as many posts as I thought I would have. Especially less added music and text than expected, - blogging apparently takes much more time than I thought ;)

At least I created an About me page that hopefully gives a bit of context.

After that came the first "real" post: a test-run combining writing, music and picture pretty much the way I intended for this blog to work. It contains a photo of my wife taking a photo of the horizon at a place close to where she grew up in Sweden called Flatruet. The picture is combined with a short guitar improvisation and a little poem. For some reason the poem ended up in Swedish which is probably not great for an blog otherwise written in English. I tried to translate but it just fell flat so I left it for now. So just disregard the poem and check out the rest here: Path fading away.

The page following that is just two comic-like doodled characters taken from my note-book. They are discussing what's coming up on the next page: So what's on the next page.

The last page added before this newsletter contains a photo from a forest walk this week-end. The trees are naked with twisting branches covered in snow. It looked like a black and white photo even in reality! Snowy, tangled forest.

Thanks for reading!
