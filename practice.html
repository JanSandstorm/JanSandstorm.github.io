PRACTICE

None of the areas have a defined deliverable or timeline — they're ongoing inquiries.

The newsletters are where I write about what's actually happening.

Writing

Literary fiction and poetry in Swedish.

Trygga i Granlyckan (completed) — A woman and her dog encounter a stranger on a forest trail, and a small incident unravels an entire community's quiet history of exclusion and self-deception.

Tjugonde november (completed) — Commandant Horn defends Narva against the tsar's overwhelming army in the autumn of 1700. A story of resolve, duty, and the moment hope returns in an impossible situation.

Trädgårdsmästaren (work in progress) — A poet discovers that time starts and stops.

Comics

I find comics fascinating as a medium — The potential for advanced story telling with scarce resources is unique.

Making comics is also extremely time consuming. So far I have made one: Little Eagle Down (black and white, 2025).

The Void (work in progress): A journalist discovers secrets about the diamond he was given — and about the woman who gave it.

Painting and drawing

I paint with acrylic on canvas. Most paintings are shared on this site.

I also practice drawing: perspective, simple anatomy, composition. Sometimes adding watercolor.

Music

The ambition is original composition — pursuing what Chopin understood about structure and applying it to the guitar's nature. The daily work: music theory, ear training, looping, repertoire built on my own arrangements.

Reading

I treat reading as a serious part of the practice, not as leisure. I try to log my books on GoodReads — you are welcome to connect or just check out what I'm currently reading.
